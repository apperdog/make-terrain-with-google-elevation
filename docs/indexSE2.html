slideshow Effects Guide Script Home
Dynamic materials for making animations on your slide of slideshow effects.
Features:
  Wheel Effects:
   Creates a texture slide transitions. Translate one texture A to texture B with a wheel effect .

  Color-Run:
   Add a color changing light on the main texture or camera view.

  Halftone Mask
   Texture slide transitions with a halftone effect.There are two types of halftone effects, moving a halftone mask and points popup in grids.

  Popup and Rotate:
   Make a texture zoomed and rotate.

  Texture Mask:
   Texture slide transitions with a mask texture, use alpha channel or grayscale.

  Grids:
   Translate a texture in gridsl.

  Triangular Billboard
   Analog a shutter or triangular billboard (Trivision).

  Bar Swipe:
   Erase or swipe texture in bar or lines.

  Texture Offset:
   Offset the texture make an infinite cyclic background.

  Sketch:
   Add a sketch texture to the main texture, with the grayscale or color as mask.

  Pixelated:
    Basic effects to make a texture Pixelated.

  Paint:
    As an oil paint filter, draw textures in oil paint style.

Also include AnimationEffectHelper and SlideshowHelper to make a slideshow with many pictures.
